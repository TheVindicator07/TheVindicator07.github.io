History

Polynesian Settlers Discover NZ

Date:

approx. 1300

About:

It is estimated that around 1300 A.D was the time when travellers from Polynesia (Rarotonga, Cook Islands and possibly Tahiti) made their way to New Zealand and began to form the cultural roots that we know as Maori culture today, such as: Tribes (Iwi), Kapa Haka (Originally a war dance, now a group performance) and Whakairo (Bone Carving).
